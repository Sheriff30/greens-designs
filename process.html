    THE PROCESS

    FORM IS A BIPRODUCT OF FUNCTION, INTENT, VISION, AND VALUES

    Systems and process combined with due-diligence, acquired knowledge and creativity, formulate consistency and value.

    STEP 1

    CONNECT

    The process is consistent for every client, whether you're looking to create a new restaurant concept or a 200 unit mixed-use urban infill development. It all starts with a meeting to get an understanding of what you are looking for and how we can align our creativity and expertise with your vision.

    Talk to Us

    STEP 2

    GENERATE

    Based on the communicated intent and understanding of the project and pursued direction of the design, we'll get to the drawing board. We focus heavily on client values and consider how we can effectively cultivate the product you've desired, while overall exceeding expectations through dynamic thought-out design solutions with photo-realistic visualizations.

    STEP 3

    MANAGE

    We understand that the process of design and building is complex and lengthy, and in most practices, the systems behind them are outdated. GREENS Designs has invested heavily in pushing technological advancements in all the nuances of the industry. As a client, you will be provided access to a client portal where transparent schedules, communication, billing and more are populated through a series of automations to alleviate the stress of wondering where the project currently stands.

    STEP 4

    CREATE

    With thorough due-diligence, an effective and responsive design solution, and our implemented communication and management systems, we've streamlined the creation and production through efficiencies in global support. GREENS Designs sees the whole project through from kick-off meeting through completed construction including all permitting and coordination services.
